About Us

Pharmaline is an online pharmacy based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We provides online ordering and delivery of prescription and non-prescription medications.

How We Started

The idea of Pharmaline was born after we noticed people losing their loved ones without getting proper medication due to the lack of adequate information on where they can get the prescribed medicines. We are here to solve the gap between patients and pharmacies.

We Are Trusted Company

We at Pharmalines provide a well-organized platform for pharmacies and patients, as we only accept pharmacies with a valid license and registered drugs through the Ethiopian Food and Drug Administration.
